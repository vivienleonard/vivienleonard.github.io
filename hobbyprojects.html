trace moi

I've stopped it after 6 months because of the lack of visits, and as a consequence, there is only 1 example restaurant on it currently.

The website is implemented with Ruby on Rails.

4shops

full software solution for retail shops

  2012

The problematic I wanted to solve for retail groups was to offer a full software solution to replace what they have today: multiple software providers with loss of data in between. By having a unique software provider, it would be possible to ease the visibility of data, and enhance innovation, with the benefit of doing it a lower cost.

With a small team, we've tried an Orange contest in december 2012 with title Fiber & 4G for startups and the following brochure: download.

We had a prototype of back-end being a web application, and a point of sale on an iPad.

The project was stopped being to big to achieve with the size of the team we had, and because of the long cycle of client acquisition in this domain, which was an issue without investors in the loop.
